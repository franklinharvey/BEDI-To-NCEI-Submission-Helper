If your output contains incorrect dates please try the following:

1. Open your target excel file
2. On the first sheet, select columns 1 and 2 (by clicking on the letter "A", holding shift, and clicking on the letter "B")
3. In the top bar, click "format"
4. Click "Cells"
5. Click "Text"

You may need to re-enter the dates into the excel file.
